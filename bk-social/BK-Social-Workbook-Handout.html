02
The DM Engine

Reel earns the comment. Automation opens the DM. You walk through the door.

Auto-DM #1 (fires the moment they comment)

▸ Auto-DM Script Template
Hi [first name] 👋

Here's the [lead magnet name] you asked for: [link]

Quick question — are you looking to [buy/sell] in the next 90 days, or just gathering info right now?

Both are great answers, just want to know how to be helpful.
Why this works: "Both are great answers" removes pressure to perform. "Want to know how to be helpful" positions you as a guide, not a salesperson. ~60% reply rate. ~25% are 90-day-active.

3-Message Follow-Up Sequence (when they reply)

MSG 1 · CURIOSITY

Ask about them

React + follow-up that's about them.
"You mentioned Frisco — is there a school district pulling you that direction?"
MSG 2 · VALUE

Useful · No pitch

Share something a friend would thank you for.
"FYI — homes in that ZIP that closed last month averaged 4 days on market. Tight inventory still."
MSG 3 · SOFT OFFER

Specific, low-commitment

Demonstrates value before asking.
"Want me to put together a quick rundown of 3 neighborhoods that fit? Takes me 30 min."
Critical rule: Never ask "do you have an agent?" That's amateur. Demonstrate value first. The relationship sells the agent role for you.

Setting it up — three free paths

All three run on Meta's official API. None require app registration, API keys, or developer setup. Pick by use case.

Quick prerequisite: Your IG must be a Business or Creator account (free switch in IG settings → Account type) and linked to a Facebook Business Page. If you don't have a connected Page, create one first — takes 2 minutes at facebook.com/pages/create.

PATH 1 · NATIVE FREE · For "DM me X" CTAs

Meta Business Suite

Triggers on incoming DMs that contain a keyword. Genuinely 100% native — Meta's own dashboard, no third-party.
  1. Go to business.facebook.com on desktop (Custom Keywords aren't available on mobile).
  2. Log in with the Facebook account connected to your IG.
  3. Click Inbox in the left menu.
  4. Click Automations at the top right.
  5. Click Custom Keywords.
  6. Name the automation, then check Instagram as the channel.
  7. Add up to 5 trigger keywords (e.g. GAME PLAN, GUIDE).
  8. Write the auto-reply (use the script from previous page).
  9. Save.
CTA pattern: Caption ends with "DM me 'GAME PLAN' and I'll send it over." When someone DMs that word, your auto-reply fires.
PATH 2 · INDUSTRY STANDARD · For "Comment X" CTAs

ManyChat (free tier)

Most popular comment-to-DM tool. Free up to 1,000 contacts. Meta-approved partner.
  1. Go to manychat.com → click Get Started Free.
  2. Sign in with Facebook (uses OAuth — no app registration, no API keys).
  3. Connect your IG Business account when prompted.
  4. Click Automation → New Automation → choose Instagram Comments.
  5. Pick the specific post or reel (or "any post").
  6. Set keyword(s) (e.g. GAME PLAN).
  7. Build the auto-DM in the visual flow builder.
  8. Activate.
CTA pattern: Caption ends with "Comment 'GAME PLAN' and I'll send you the full guide." Comment fires the DM. Free tier covers 90%+ of agents.
PATH 3 · BROKERAGE · Full CRM pipeline

GHL (GoHighLevel)

Already paid at the brokerage. Adds CRM tagging, multi-step nurture, and lead routing on top of the DM trigger.
  1. Connect your IG Business profile (must be linked to a Facebook Business Page).
  2. Go to Automation → Workflows → Create New.
  3. Trigger: Instagram Comment (or DM, depending on CTA).
  4. Filter: keyword match (e.g. GAME PLAN).
  5. Action 1: Send IG DM with your script.
  6. Action 2: Add tag to contact (e.g. "IG-GamePlan-Lead").
  7. Action 3: Add to nurture campaign or assign to you.
  8. Save and activate.
Bonus: Multiple keywords per post can route to different DM flows and different lead segments.
Compliance note: Meta's API allows one automated message in response to a comment, with a 7-day window to send it. After that first DM, follow-ups require the user to reply first. This is why your auto-DM #1 ends with a question — it earns the right to keep the conversation going.

04
Reach Multipliers Checklist

Apply these to every reel. Most agents skip them. The ones who don't, win.

Use native Instagram captions — not CapCut, not VideoLeap, not Premiere
Algorithm reads native text → better distribution. Burned-in captions read as graphics.
Tag specific neighborhood — not the metro
"Lakewood, Dallas" or "Westover Hills, Fort Worth" — not just "Dallas, TX"
Add a collaborator tag — lender, photographer, stager, title rep, local business
Reel posts to BOTH accounts simultaneously. Roughly doubles your reach.
Limit hashtags to 3–5 specific ones
Hashtag dumps are no longer a distribution signal. Search keywords in caption matter more.
Write captions with searchable phrases
"first-time homebuyer Frisco" — words your audience actually types into Instagram search
One clear DM CTA per reel
"Comment 'GAME PLAN' and I'll send you the full guide." Specific trigger word matters.
Hook in first 2 seconds — no "Hey guys"
Open with the punchiest line of the script. Throat-clearing kills retention.
05
5 Content Pillars (with topic ideas)

Pick the pillar first. Topic comes second. Rotation beats variety.

01 · Market Authority

You. On camera. Real numbers. 30 seconds.
  • "Frisco home prices Q1: up 4.2%. Here's what it means."
  • "Austin's 78704 zip just hit a new median. The shift you should know."
  • "Fort Worth inventory just doubled. What that means for buyers."

02 · Education

Explain something complex in simple words.
  • "What an option fee actually is in Texas — and why $150 protects $500K."
  • "The difference between pre-qualified and pre-approved (and why it matters)."
  • "3 things that can kill a deal at closing — and how to avoid all 3."

03 · Hyperlocal

Content nobody else has. Beats listing demos every time.
  • "4 best taco shops in Bishop Arts — and the listing under $500K walking distance to all 4."
  • "5 hidden parks in Cedar Park your kids will actually use."
  • "Best coffee in McKinney + the school district pulling families in."

04 · Behind the Curtain

Show the work nobody sees. Authenticity converts.
  • "3 things I do the morning of every closing."
  • "How I prep a listing the day before it goes active."
  • "The conversation I have with every buyer at offer time."

05 · Social Proof

Don't ask "how was your experience." Ask better questions.
  • Q: "What was stopping you from buying before we worked together?"
  • Q: "What surprised you most about the process?"
  • Q: "What would you say to someone considering working with us?"
