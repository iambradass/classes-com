HOOK
ENGINEERING

Stop the scroll. Earn the second.

The first frame

How to know your hook is failing

→ Views below your follower count

→ Watch time under 30%

→ Zero shares

What a working hook looks like

→ Views 5–50× follower count

→ Watch time over 60%

→ Saves and shares ratio > likes

5 hook formulas that work for agents

Plug your topic into one of these five patterns. Don't reinvent.

01"3 mistakes [buyers/sellers] are making right now in [DFW/Austin] — and how to avoid them"FORMULA: Mistake call-out + audience + location
02"If I were [buying/selling] a house in [Frisco/Round Rock] today, here's exactly what I'd do"FORMULA: "If I were" + specific action + hyperlocal
03"One strategy that's saving my clients $8,000+ in closing costs right now"FORMULA: Specific dollar amount + outcome + timeframe
04"What actually happens on closing day — in 30 seconds"FORMULA: Insider explainer + time promise
05"The one thing every first-time buyer in [city] gets wrong about pre-approval"FORMULA: Niche audience + contrarian truth + specific topic

What kills a hook

Every one of these I've watched an agent do this week.

❌ Hook killers

"Hey guys, what's up..." — algorithm already moved on

Slow zoom on exterior — no tension, no information

"My name is ___ and I'm with ___" — they don't care yet

Trending audio with no payoff — they came for the song

"Did you know that..." — pure throat-clearing

✓ Hook winners

Specific number in first 2 sec — "$8,000 saved"

Direct address — "If you're buying in DFW..."

Contrarian claim — "Stop using a buyer's agent like this"

Visual pattern interrupt — text overlay that demands a read

Curiosity gap — "The one room buyers always check first"

STRONG

The first DM (auto-send script)

Delivers the magnet · ends with a question · sounds like a human

Auto-DM #1 — sent the moment they comment
Hi [first name] 👋

Here's the [lead magnet] you asked for: [link]

Quick question — are you looking to buy in the next 90 days, or just gathering info right now?

Both are great answers, just want to know how to be helpful.

Why "both are great"

Removes the pressure to perform. They tell you the truth.

Why the timeline question

90 days = active lead. Info-only = nurture into your CRM.

Response rate

~60% reply. ~25% are 90-day-active. Math works.

The 3-message follow-up sequence

No pitch. No "do you have an agent?" Build the relationship in 3 moves.

MESSAGE 1 · CURIOSITY

React to what they said. Ask a follow-up that's about them.

"You mentioned Frisco — is there a school district pulling you that direction?"

MESSAGE 2 · VALUE

Share something genuinely useful. No pitch.

"FYI — homes in that ZIP that closed last month averaged 4 days on market. Tight inventory still."

MESSAGE 3 · SOFT OFFER

A specific, low-commitment ask that demonstrates value.

"Want me to put together a quick rundown of 3 neighborhoods that fit? Takes me 30 min."

You never asked "do you have an agent?" — you demonstrated value instead.

Section 03 · 10 minutes

REACH
MULTIPLIERS

Three things most agents skip.

Multiplier #1: Native captions, not CapCut

Adam Mosseri (head of Instagram) confirmed this directly.

❌ CapCut burned-in captions

Algorithm sees: graphics

Algorithm reads content: no

Distribution boost: none

Same with VideoLeap, Premiere, all third-party caption tools.

✓ Instagram native captions

Algorithm sees: text

Algorithm reads content: yes

Distribution boost: significant

White text + black highlight = highest readability.

2 seconds to fix. Permanent reach gain.

Multipliers #2 & #3: Two tags. Massive reach.

📍 Location Tag

Instagram serves your reel to people who follow, search, or browse content tied to that location.

Tactic: Tag the specific neighborhood, not the metro.

  • ❌ "Dallas, TX"
  • ✓ "Lakewood, Dallas"
  • ✓ "Bishop Arts District"
  • ✓ "Westover Hills, Fort Worth"

@ Collaborator Tag

Reel posts to both accounts simultaneously. Same content, two feeds, double the audience.

Who to collab with:

  • → Your lender (cross-promote)
  • → Your stager / photographer
  • → A local business in the neighborhood
  • → Your title rep (yes — me)

30 seconds per reel · Roughly doubles your reach

The hashtag truth most agents miss

❌ The 30-hashtag dump

#realtor #realestate #dfw #fortworth #dallasrealestate #firsttimehomebuyer #homesforsale #realestateagent #milliondollarlisting #luxuryhomes...

Looks like spam. Doesn't help reach. Algorithm has moved on.

✓ 3–5 specific tags + keyword caption

#FortWorthRealEstate #LakewoodDallas #DFWHomes

Then write the caption with searchable words: "first-time homebuyer Frisco," "Lakewood Dallas listings under $600K," etc.

Caption keywords now matter more than hashtags. Write for search.
